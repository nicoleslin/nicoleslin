Hello, World!

I'm Nicole.


I'm an engineer with a knack for getting my hands dirty, whether that be from exploring the outdoors or building cars as part of Cornell University's Electric Formula Racing Team. As somebody who loves to do, to problem solve, and to create, I thrive in fast-paced and challenging work environments. I have over six years of experience integrating mechanical, electrical, and software systems, and am aiming to take my interdisciplinary background in robotics and embedded systems to contribute meaningfully to a team building impactful products.

Apart from my technical interests, I enjoy being outside, trying new things, and traveling. I especially love to hike, camp, and climb, which, lucky for me, is plentiful where I go to school in Ithaca, NY. In my free time, you can also find me reading, hammocking, or reading in a hammock. I'm always on the lookout for book and podcast recommendations, as well as new baking recipes to satisfy my sweet tooth!
